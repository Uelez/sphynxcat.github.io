Money While You Sleep?

6% Buyback and 4% Marketing

Sphynx Cat collects 6% Buyback and 4% Marketing tax on each transaction and maintains it under contract. Whenever a Sell happens, a fraction of the Buyback amount is used to automatically buy from the Liquidity Pool and burns those tokens accordingly.

1% Distribution Rewards

1% of each transaction is distributed to all Sphynx Cat Holders, by doing this you simply allow the Sphynx Cat to slowly but steadily increase in your wallet without doing anything.
